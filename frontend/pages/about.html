Our Story

Born from a
Love of Craft

DURGAS was founded in 2008 by master jeweller Kavita Mehta, whose family has been crafting jewellery for over four generations in Mumbai's historic Zaveri Bazaar. What began as a single workshop has grown into a beloved luxury brand serving discerning clients across India.

Every piece in our collection is the result of hundreds of hours of design, craftsmanship, and finishing. We source only certified, ethically-mined gemstones, and our gold and metals meet the highest international standards of purity.

At DURGAS, we believe jewellery is not merely an accessory — it is a memory, an heirloom, a story worn close to the heart.

What We Stand For

Our Values

◇

Uncompromising Quality

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality checks. We use only BIS Hallmarked gold, IGI-certified diamonds, and ethically sourced gemstones in all our creations.

✦

Artisanal Heritage

Our 12 master craftsmen carry traditions passed down through generations. Each piece is hand-finished with patience, precision, and profound pride in the craft.

♡

Ethical Practices

From sourcing to delivery, we are committed to sustainability and ethics. Our supply chain is fully traceable, and we support fair wages for all artisans in our network.
